One Hit Wonder Application

The One-Hit Wonder Application is as easy as 1, 2, 3!

Step 1: Find your time range

Use the top slider to select the time period you care about. You can choose a range including as early as 1940 and as recent as 2015.

The application will only consider songs by each artist during the selected time period, so if your favorite artist had 7 big hits spanning 1972 to 1978, but you select a time range of 1960 to 1972, only the hits in 1972 will be used to determine how big of a "One Hit Wonder" your favorite artist is.

Step 2: Weight the Importance of that "Big Hit"

To be a one hit wonder, your notoriety really depends on that one big hit, right? Use the second slider to weight how important it is the artist's fame/infamy depends on a single hit.

The more heavily you weight this factor, the more you'll see artists at the top of the list that only have one song in their arsenal.

Step 3: Weight the Importance of Overall Musical Fame

There are a ton of artists who only had one hit, but we can't even remember their names because even with their one 'hit', they're just too obscure.

Use the third and final slider to weight the overall fame into the calculation and see names you really recognize rise to the top of the list!

Have fun!

And thanks for playing :)
